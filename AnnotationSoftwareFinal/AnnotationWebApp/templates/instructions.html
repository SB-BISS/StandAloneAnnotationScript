Annotation instructions

To annotate audio files, follow the following steps:

    1. Go to the Split Files tab an upload the files you want to annotate
    2. When you've uploaded the files, click on the "Split Files" button
    3. Navigate to the "Audio Annotation" tab and here the splitted files will appear
    4. Click on each file and specify the emotion that you hear in the audiofile and click "submit"


      Notes:



      - Sometimes the splitted audiofiles are too short. When you click on such a short file in the "Audio Annotation" tab it vanishes. You can just ignore this.

      - At this stage you don't need to pay much attention to the predicted emotions as they will likely be inaccurate. Through annotating more data the model can be improved later on.
